1971
Born: Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa.
1983
Early Interest in Computers: At age 12, he sold his first software, a game called Blastar.
1995
Zip2: Elon co-founded Zip2, a software company, which was acquired by Compaq for $307 million in 1999.
1999
X.com and PayPal: Elon founded X.com, an online payment company, which later became PayPal and was acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion in 2002.
2002
SpaceX: Elon founded Space Exploration Technologies Corp. (SpaceX) with the goal of reducing space transportation costs.
2004
Tesla Motors: Elon joined Tesla Motors, Inc. as chairman of the board and later became its CEO and product architect.
2006
SolarCity: Elon co-founded SolarCity, a solar energy services company, which was later acquired by Tesla, Inc.
2015
OpenAI: Elon co-founded OpenAI, a research organization aimed at promoting and developing friendly AI.
2016
Neuralink and The Boring Company: Elon co-founded Neuralink, a neurotechnology company, and founded The Boring Company, a tunnel construction services company.
2020
SpaceX and NASA: SpaceX became the first private company to send astronauts to the International Space Station (ISS).
